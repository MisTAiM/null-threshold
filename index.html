Overview

The World Cracked Open

◈

The Voids

Massive fractures that tore open across the globe a decade ago. They bleed Echo — sentient residue from a dead universe that collapsed before ours fully formed. Echo attaches to living hosts and awakens dormant potential buried in human DNA: potential that was supposed to evolve over thousands more years.

⚡

Resonants

Those whose DNA fused with Echo. No guilds. No government registry. No ranked hierarchy. Just power, and the people who want to use it. Corporations privatized Echo extraction. Nations collapsed or reformed around Resonant supremacy. The weak don't just stay weak — they get consumed.

◻

The Null

Those Echo never touched. Or so they were told. Kairo Vess tested null three times. He spent three years surviving inside Void fractures as a diver — salvaging Echo crystals for poverty wages in the most dangerous job on earth. Most divers die in six months. Something in Kairo doesn't let go.

⟳

The Inversion

When a Void fracture in the Ashfield stops pulling matter in and starts pushing Echo-contaminated entities out into the civilian population, the corporations seal the district. They write off the people inside. Four individuals who won't accept that answer walk back in. Everything changes.
